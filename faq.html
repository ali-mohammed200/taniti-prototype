FAQ

Question
What voltage are the power outlets? Power outlets are 120 volts (the same as in the United States).
Question
When is alcohol not allowed? Alcohol is not allowed to be served or sold between the hours of midnight and 9:00 a.m.
Question
What is the drinking age and how strictly is it enforced? The drinking age on Taniti is 18 and the drinking age is not strictly enforced.
Question
What languages do the locals speak? Many younger Tanitians speak fluent English. Very little English is spoken in rural areas, especially by the older residents.
Question
Are there medical facilities? There is one hospital and several clinics. The hospital has many multilingual employees.
Question
What is the crime situation in Taniti? Violent crime is very rare in Taniti, but as tourism increases, there are more reports of pickpocketing and other petty crimes.
Question
What days are attractions closed for business? Taniti enjoys a large number of national holidays, and many tourist attractions and restaurants will be closed on holidays, so visitors should plan accordingly.
Question
What currencies are accepted? Taniti uses the U.S. dollar as its currency, but many businesses will also accept euros and yen. Several banks facilitate currency exchange, and many businesses accept major credit cards.
